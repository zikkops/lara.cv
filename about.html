My World,

I am a highly motivated individual with an optimistic personality who currently holds over 5 years of experience with clients in the Middle-East; catering to their needs as an English/Arabic copywriter, translator, and creative writer.

It’s a fact, Copywriters don’t like to talk about themselves.
We usually prefer to talk about you, your company and products,
but here’s my story…

It all started when my friends at college needed help with their projects, while I was studying Applied Linguistics, pursuing my passion for languages.

As I helped them with their projects; searching, editing, and finding the right words, I realized that I’m a struggling college student, why not start charging for my words and ideas?

Word got out that I’m good with words (and grammar, too!).

In the Spring of 2015, I was approached by a small business owner looking for help with editing and translating some documents for his advertising agency “Origin”, I applied to be their translator, but he spotted a better use for me and hired me as a copywriter.

That’s when I became a Copywriter, without even knowing I wanted to be one.

Onwards, I started advertising my skills, applying for other copywriting jobs and undertaking small freelance projects.

In 2019, I was hired as an Arabic copywriter for Omnicom Media Group to help with Majid Al Futtaim City Centre accounts in UAE, Oman, Bahrain, and Lebanon.

The psychology, the marketing, and communicating with different cultures got to me; I wanted more (Yes! I’m selfish that way), I wanted to understand more about people, their ways of thinking, their problems and how to solve them.

“Learning leads to knowledge, knowledge helps you solve problems”

This is why I ventured in the world of International Commercial Negotiations where I pursued my Masters, and OH MY, what a world; different cultures, behaviors, business ethics, traditions, relationships, communications, and ideologies.

Learning about all these new cultures made me realize how different we are, how each individual is unique, and I learned how to apply this in my copywriting.

Just like in life, in advertising each customer requires a different approach!
